🎂 Mom's Birthday Game 🎂

Help Mom catch the gifts and avoid the bad items!

Score: 0 / 100
Lives: 3

⬅️ Use LEFT and RIGHT arrow keys to move

Collect 🎁 gifts (green) to increase score

Avoid ❌ bad items (red)

  • Use arrow keys to move Mom left and right
